Programs Section — Auto-Scroll Style

How should the 4 program cards scroll/animate on the homepage?

A

Horizontal Drag Carousel

Cards sit in a horizontal scrollable row. Auto-advances every 4 seconds, pauses on hover/touch. Prev/next arrow buttons visible. Works great on mobile with swipe. Each card shows program icon, number, title, description, and tags.

B

Vertical Stacked Reveal

Cards stack vertically and animate in one by one as you scroll down the page (intersection observer). Each card slides in from the side with a slight fade. Feels more editorial and deliberate. Better for long-form reading on desktop.

C

Auto-Rotating Feature Card

One large featured card at a time, full-width. Tabs/dots at bottom for each program. Auto-rotates every 5 seconds. Clicking a tab jumps to that program. The active card shows image, description, and a "Explore" link. Very visual and immersive.
